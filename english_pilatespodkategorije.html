TRAINING DESCRIPTION

The modern way of life (sitting at the computer, physical inactivity) brings back pain and various problems with the spine and joints. The Pilates we practice today has evolved from a recreational to a therapeutic exercise program. Today, it is not uncommon for physiotherapists to advise their patients to practice Pilates.

TRAINING DESCRIPTION

The advantage of training on the reformer is in symmetry, ie equal load on the left and right sides during training. In most people, the statics of the body is unevenly developed and asymmetrical, and the division into left-handed and right-handed implies that one side of the body is more developed than the other.
