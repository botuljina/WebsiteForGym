Nutritionist

A nutritionist is a person who studies the nutritional value of food and the way in which nutrients are used in the body, as well as the connection between diet and health. If you want to lose weight or improve your health through diet, leave all questions to an expert and do not waste time, but make an appointment with a nutritionist and improve the quality of your life.

All people, regardless of gender, age and lifestyle are candidates for a nutritional examination.

Most nutritionists treat the consequence, but not the cause. One out of lack of knowledge, another out of irresponsibility, a third out of vanity. Choose carefully.

What awaits you at the examination?

  • 1) Assess your nutritional status.
  • 2) Measurement of body weight on a Taniti apparatus.
  • 3) Determination of BMI (body nutrition index)
  • 4) Blood pressure measurement, if necessary.
  • 5) Analysis of eating habits, lifestyle and health status.
