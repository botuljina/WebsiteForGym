Massages

Massage is a system of methodical hand movements, ie. grips that the therapist applies to the patient. Massage can be therapeutic, sports, relaxing, aromatic anti-cellulite, etc. It has a direct mechanical effect on the tissues of the human body. Make sure that the massage is performed correctly only by a professional with practiced movements, knowledge of anatomy and body problems related to massage.

Under the influence of massage, local metabolism and elimination of decomposed products increases.

Under the influence of massage, blood circulation increases and fatigue decreases.
