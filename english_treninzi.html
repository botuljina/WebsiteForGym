Yoga

What type of yoga suits you best? You didn't even know what all kinds of yoga are, how they differ, how any approach can help us in this world of fast pace, agitated thoughts and unfulfilled emotions.

Yoga distinguishes five basic energies that govern the various activities of the body.

Pilates

If you want to tighten your muscles and fix the line, and at the same time learn the correct one Breathing and holding, Pilates is the right thing for you. This type of fitness helps relieve stress and contributes to relaxation, and you can also practice it at home

It helps to shape your body and make your muscles spindle-shaped.

Core

Glute & Core training is your basis for a strong body and its goal is to strengthen, as the name says, your core muscles - the ones that support the spine. Training will suit everyone, regardless of whether you have just started training or training is your daily routine.

When your core is strong, everyday responsibilities become much easier.

Cardio

Cardio training is the most effective way to remove fat deposits, achieve and maintain fitness. Cardio exercises are necessary for any effective training program because they encourage fat burning, but they are also good for the overall health of the body. Also, if you haven't exercised for a long time, and you don't have the strength for other workouts, you can always adjust the cardio workout to your abilities.

Cardio exercises speed up your metabolism, strengthen your heart and lungs, and reduce your resting heart rate.
